Instructions

1. Click on the "+Sleep" button to add a recommended 8 hours of sleep to your schedule.

2. If you are planning a school day, click on the "+School" button to add 7 hours of school.

3. Click on "+Task" to begin adding your tasks for the day.

3. Within the Task Form, add a short description of your task, then enter the duration of your event in hours, and then indicate the priority of the task by entering a number between 1 and 5 (1 being the most important and 5 being the least important).

4. Click on the "Add" button, so that the task can be registered.

5. Once you see a check mark appear, press "Clear" and continue adding events.

6. Once you finish adding your tasks, click on the "Plan" button.

7. If your tasks exceed 24 hours, a button will appear that says "Remove Lowest Priority". Click on that button and then click on "Plan".

8. Once your tasks can fit within 24 hours, your schedule will appear!
